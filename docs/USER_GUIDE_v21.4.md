## GETTING STARTED

### Login
1. Open https://procurbosse.edgeone.app in your browser
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In** — you'll go straight to your dashboard
4. For access issues, contact your IT administrator

### Your Dashboard
After login you see coloured module tiles based on your role:
- **Blue** = Procurement · **Purple** = Finance · **Teal** = Inventory
- **Green** = Quality · **Orange** = Suppliers · **Red** = Admin
- Click any tile to open that module

---

## PROCUREMENT

### Creating a Requisition
1. Click **Procurement** tile → **Requisitions**
2. Click **+ New Requisition**
3. Fill: Title, Department, Items (name + quantity + price), Justification
4. Click **Submit** — manager gets notified via SMS

### Purchase Orders
- Created from approved requisitions or manually
- Path: Procurement → Purchase Orders
- Actions: Approve, Send to Supplier, Mark Delivered

### Goods Received (GRN)
- When goods arrive: Procurement → Goods Received → + New GRN
- Link to the Purchase Order, enter quantities received
- Triggers 3-way match for payment

---

## FINANCE

### Payment Vouchers
- Finance → Vouchers → Payment
- Create voucher, attach invoice, submit for approval
- Approved vouchers trigger payment processing

### Budgets
- Finance → Budgets
- View budget vs actual spend per department
- Request budget override if needed

---

## COMMUNICATIONS

### Sending SMS
1. Click **Communications** tile → **SMS**
2. Enter phone number (Kenya: 07XX or +254)
3. Type message → **Send SMS**
4. WhatsApp: recipient must first send `join bad-machine` to +14155238886

### Email
1. Communications → **Email**
2. Click **Compose** (bottom-left)
3. Fill To, Subject, Body → **Send**

---

## KIOSK (No Login Required)
For departments without system accounts:
1. Open the **Kiosk App** on the department terminal
2. Enter your name and department
3. Add items needed with quantities
4. Click **Submit Request** — SMS sent to Procurement Manager instantly

## TROUBLESHOOTING

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Page won't load | Press Ctrl+Shift+R (hard refresh) |
| Stuck on loading | Wait 10s, then hard refresh |
| Access denied | Your IP may be blocked — contact IT |
| Can't see a module | Your role may not include it — contact admin |
| SMS not sending | Check number format: +254XXXXXXXXX |
